01

Call Us at

You will get connected to our Care Coordinator who will confirm information and review your condition as well as collect past medical history.

02

Our Care coordinator will connect you to the Psychiatrist.

The Psychiatrist will speak to you using secure computer technology and it is as similar as an in-person appointment.

03

Get easily accessible and immediate care.

Pyschiatrist will put your notes in electronic health records. Our care coordinator then will review diagnosed instructions, schedule follow-up visits if necessary and send a secure email with visit summary and follow-ups.
